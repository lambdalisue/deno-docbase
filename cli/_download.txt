Download an attachment file.

Usage: docbase download <DOMAIN> <ATTACHMENT> [OPTIONS]

Arguments:
  DOMAIN                Domain name of the team
  ATTACHMENT            Attachment ID or URL to download

Options:
  -h, --help            Show help

Examples:
  docbase download my-domain unique-uuid-value.md

  docbase download my-domain https://docbase.io/file_attachments/unique-uuid-value.md
