Get a group.

Usage: docbase group <DOMAIN> <GROUP> [OPTIONS]

Arguments:
  DOMAIN                Domain name of the team
  GROUP                 Group ID or URL to get

Options:
  --json                Output as JSON
  -h, --help            Show help

Examples:
  docbase group my-domain 1234

  docbase group my-domain https://{domain}.docbase.io/groups/1234
