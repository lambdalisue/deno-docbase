Unarchive a memo.

Usage: docbase unarchive <DOMAIN> <MEMO> [OPTIONS]

Arguments:
  DOMAIN                Domain name of the team
  MEMO                  Memo ID or URL to unarchive

Options:
  -h, --help            Show help

Examples:
  docbase unarchive my-domain 12345

  docbase unarchive my-domain https://{domain}.docbase.io/posts/12345
